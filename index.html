Welcome to Irfa Henna

Based in Austin, Leander Texas, with over a decade of experience in the art of henna, I specialize in creating intricate and beautiful designs that celebrate your special moments. From traditional bridal mehndi to contemporary patterns and elegant Arabic designs, each creation is crafted with passion, precision, and pure natural henna.

My journey with henna began as a childhood fascination and has blossomed into a professional career serving clients across Austin and surrounding areas for weddings, festivals, events, and special celebrations. I offer expertise in Indian traditional, Pakistani, Arabic Gulf styles, and modern fusion designs. I believe that henna is more than just body art – it's a celebration of culture, tradition, and individual beauty.
